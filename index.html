Gregory Moomjy

Musicology Journalist & Dramaturge


Gregory Moomjy is a Musicologist, Journalist, Artistic Director, and Dramaturge in New York City.

As a Journalist, he covers Opera, Classical Music, and Disability.

He is the Co-Founder and Artistic Director of Opera Praktikos, the first Disability Affirmative Opera Company in New York.
